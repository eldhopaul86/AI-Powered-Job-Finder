About SmartHire

SmartHire is an AI-driven job-matching platform designed to connect skilled professionals with the right career opportunities. By analyzing resumes and job descriptions, our intelligent system provides a similarity score, ensuring precise and relevant job recommendations.

Our mission is to streamline hiring by making the process faster and more efficient for both job seekers and employers. SmartHire offers resume analysis, AI-powered job matching, and career guidance to help individuals grow in their professions.

Whether you're looking for your first job or aiming for career advancement, SmartHire simplifies your journey towards success. With SmartHire, upload your resume, find the perfect job, and get hired with ease!
